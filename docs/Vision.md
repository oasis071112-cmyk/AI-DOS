# Vision

## Mission

Teach AI to think like a world-class design team before generating interfaces.

---

## Vision

Build the open standard for AI-native product design.

AI-DOS aims to become the knowledge system that enables AI agents to understand design, user experience, engineering, and product thinking.

---

## Why AI-DOS Exists

Today's AI can generate interfaces.

But it does not truly understand design.

AI-DOS exists to teach AI:

- How to think before designing.
- How to make design decisions.
- How to build consistent design systems.
- How to create products instead of random pages.

---

## Core Principles

- Design is a system, not decoration.
- Motion communicates, not entertains.
- Whitespace is part of the design.
- Consistency builds trust.
- Every pixel has a purpose.
- Simplicity is powerful.

---

## Long-term Goal

Become the open-source Design Operating System for AI Agents.

Support any AI platform, any design tool, and any frontend framework.

---

## Success

AI no longer generates beautiful interfaces.

AI designs world-class digital products.
