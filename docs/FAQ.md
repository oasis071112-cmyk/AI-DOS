# Frequently Asked Questions

## What is AI-DOS?

AI-DOS is an open-source Design Operating System for AI Agents.

It teaches AI how to think about design instead of simply generating interfaces.

---

## Is AI-DOS a Design System?

No.

A Design System focuses on reusable UI components.

AI-DOS is a broader knowledge framework that includes design philosophy, UX, engineering, review processes, and design patterns.

---

## Is AI-DOS a Prompt Library?

No.

Prompts are only one part of the project.

The core of AI-DOS is its knowledge base.

---

## Who can use AI-DOS?

Anyone building digital products with AI.

This includes designers, developers, startups, and AI engineers.

---

## Which AI models are supported?

AI-DOS is model-agnostic.

It can be adapted for ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Cursor, Codex, and other AI systems.

---

## Can I contribute?

Yes.

Community contributions are welcome.

Please follow the contribution guide before submitting pull requests.

---

## What is the long-term goal?

To become the open standard for AI-native product design.
