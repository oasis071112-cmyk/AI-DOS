# Design Principles

## Purpose

Design Principles define the fundamental rules that guide every design decision in AI-DOS.

They ensure consistency across products, interfaces, and user experiences.

---

## Principle 1 — User First

Every design decision must prioritize the user's needs over visual decoration.

If an element does not improve the user experience, remove it.

---

## Principle 2 — Clarity Before Beauty

A clear interface is more valuable than a beautiful interface.

Users should understand what to do within seconds.

---

## Principle 3 — Simplicity Wins

Remove unnecessary elements.

Reduce cognitive load.

Keep only what provides value.

---

## Principle 4 — Consistency Creates Trust

Use consistent layouts, spacing, colors, typography, and interactions.

Predictability improves usability.

---

## Principle 5 — Hierarchy Guides Attention

Users should immediately know:

- What is most important.
- What to read first.
- What action to take next.

---

## Principle 6 — Motion Has Purpose

Animation should communicate.

Never animate for decoration.

Motion should explain, guide, or provide feedback.

---

## Principle 7 — Accessibility Is Not Optional

Design should work for everyone.

Accessibility is part of good design, not an afterthought.

---

## AI Rule

Before producing any design, verify that every principle is respected.
