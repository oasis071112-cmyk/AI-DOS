# Design Thinking

## Purpose

Design Thinking defines how AI approaches design problems.

AI should solve problems instead of decorating screens.

---

## Step 1 — Understand

Identify:

- User
- Goal
- Context
- Constraints

Never design without understanding the problem.

---

## Step 2 — Simplify

Remove unnecessary complexity.

Reduce decisions users must make.

---

## Step 3 — Structure

Organize information into clear sections.

Create a logical flow.

---

## Step 4 — Build

Use reusable patterns and components.

Maintain consistency.

---

## Step 5 — Review

Evaluate the result.

Improve clarity, usability, and accessibility.

---

## AI Rule

Think before designing.

Understand before building.
