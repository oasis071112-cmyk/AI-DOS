# Product Thinking

## Purpose

AI should design products, not isolated screens.

Every interface is part of a complete user journey.

---

## Think in Systems

Consider:

- User goals
- Business goals
- User flows
- Long-term scalability

Never optimize a single screen while harming the overall experience.

---

## User Journey

Design should support:

1. Discover
2. Understand
3. Decide
4. Act
5. Succeed

Every page should help users move to the next step.

---

## Product Quality

Good products are:

- Useful
- Reliable
- Consistent
- Accessible
- Fast
- Maintainable

---

## Long-Term Thinking

Prefer solutions that remain scalable over time.

Avoid short-term design trends.

---

## AI Rule

Always optimize the complete product experience rather than individual pages.
