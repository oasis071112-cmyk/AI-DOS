# Decision Framework

## Purpose

This framework helps AI make consistent design decisions.

---

## Decision Priority

When multiple solutions exist, prioritize:

1. User Experience
2. Information Hierarchy
3. Accessibility
4. Consistency
5. Performance
6. Visual Design

Never sacrifice usability for aesthetics.

---

## Decision Questions

Before adding any element, ask:

- Does it solve a problem?
- Does it improve understanding?
- Does it reduce effort?
- Is it consistent?
- Is it necessary?

If the answer is "No", remove it.

---

## Trade-offs

Prefer:

- Simple over complex.
- Clear over clever.
- Fast over flashy.
- Consistent over unique.

---

## AI Rule

Every design decision must have a purpose.
