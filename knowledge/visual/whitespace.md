# Whitespace

## Purpose

Whitespace improves readability and focus.

Empty space is an active design element.

---

## Principles

Whitespace should:

- Separate ideas
- Reduce cognitive load
- Create rhythm
- Improve readability

---

## Spacing Strategy

Increase spacing between major sections.

Reduce spacing inside related groups.

Create consistent visual rhythm.

---

## Avoid

Avoid filling every empty area.

Empty space is intentional.

---

## AI Rule

Whitespace is content.

Do not remove space to fit more elements.
