# Depth

## Purpose

Depth creates visual layers without adding complexity.

Interfaces should feel dimensional but lightweight.

---

## Methods

Use:

- Soft shadows
- Layered surfaces
- Gentle gradients
- Blur only when meaningful

---

## Layering

Background

↓

Surface

↓

Card

↓

Interactive Element

↓

Modal

Each level should be visually distinguishable.

---

## Avoid

Avoid:

- Heavy shadows
- Excessive blur
- Unrealistic lighting
- Fake 3D effects

---

## AI Rule

Depth should improve structure, never distract.
