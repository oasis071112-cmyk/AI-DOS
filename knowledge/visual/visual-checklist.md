# Visual Checklist

Before finalizing a design, verify:

## Clarity

- Is the primary action obvious?
- Is the page easy to scan?

---

## Hierarchy

- Is there a clear visual order?
- Is the headline dominant?

---

## Layout

- Is spacing consistent?
- Is alignment correct?

---

## Balance

- Is the composition balanced?
- Is whitespace used effectively?

---

## Color

- Is the palette restrained?
- Is contrast sufficient?

---

## Motion

- Does motion support understanding?

---

## Accessibility

- Is text readable?
- Are interactive elements obvious?

---

## AI Rule

If any item fails, improve the design before completing the task.
