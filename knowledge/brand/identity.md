# Brand Tone

## Purpose

Tone defines how products communicate through text.

---

## Writing Style

Use language that is:

- Clear
- Friendly
- Professional
- Direct
- Helpful

---

## Avoid

Avoid writing that feels:

- Robotic
- Overly technical
- Marketing-heavy
- Aggressive
- Vague

---

## UX Copy Principles

Write short, actionable messages.

Examples:

Good:

- Create Project
- Save Changes
- Continue
- Learn More

Avoid:

- Click Here
- Submit
- Proceed
- Confirm Action

---

## Error Messages

Explain:

1. What happened.
2. Why it happened.
3. How to fix it.

Never blame the user.

---

## Empty States

Always explain:

- What this page is.
- Why it is empty.
- What users can do next.

---

## AI Rule

Every piece of text should reduce cognitive load and help users make the next decision.
